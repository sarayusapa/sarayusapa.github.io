MUSIC

music has always been central to my life. i’ve been a student of carnatic music vocals and violin, and it's the one thing i hold closest to my heart.

i believe that carnatic music is the one of the most sophisticated and nuanced art forms ever created. it demands both intellect and emotion, and that’s what makes it so powerful, even if not always understood or appreciated widely. someday, i hope to perform and share it in a way that does justice to its beauty.

alongside my formal training, i’ve been exploring music on my own. i've played around with the keyboard since i was little, lately trying my hand at guitar, and experimenting with blending classical and electronic sounds on logic pro and the geoshred app.

once in a while, i post classical-based covers on my tiny little youtube channel.
